⚙ Instagram Session Setup

InstaGet needs your Instagram sessionid cookie to download media. Get it from any browser:

  1. Open instagram.com in Chrome, Firefox, or Safari and log in
  2. Open DevTools: F12 (desktop) or Share → Request Desktop Site then F12
  3. Go to Application (Chrome) / Storage (Firefox) tab
  4. Click Cookies → https://www.instagram.com
  5. Copy the value of sessionid (and optionally csrftoken)

On Render: also add INSTAGRAM_SESSION_ID as an environment variable in your Render dashboard so it survives redeploys.
